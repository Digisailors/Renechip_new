Current career opportunities at Renechip

Ready to shape the digital future? Take the leap and join us on this exciting journey!

We are seeking 3D Modeling and Design professionals tasked with the development of intricate three-dimensional representations using specialized software. In this position, individuals will immerse themselves in crafting intricate virtual models that replicate physical objects in various environments. They will employ an array of techniques and tools in the realm of 3D Modeling to define shapes, textures, and, where relevant, the dynamic behaviors of digital elements.

We are seeking Product Development and Engineering professionals to lead the process of creating innovative products. In this role, you will play a key part in every stage of product development, working closely with diverse teams to design, prototype, and refine products for successful market launch.

We are currently seeking CAD Drafting and Documentation professionals who will be responsible for creating precise technical drawings and comprehensive documentation essential for the product development and engineering process. In this role, individuals will utilize Computer-Aided Design (CAD) software to translate conceptual designs into detailed, accurate blueprints and schematics.

In this role, individuals will employ reverse engineering techniques to unravel the design, functionality, and components of complex structures when original documentation is unavailable or incomplete.

We are currently seeking professionals with expertise in Prototyping and Rapid Manufacturing, integral components of the product development process. In this position, individuals will be instrumental in translating ideas into tangible prototypes and streamlining rapid manufacturing processes.
