Current career opportunities at Renechip

Ready to shape the digital future? Apply now and join us on this incredible adventure!

We are seeking 3D Modeling and Design professionals tasked with the development of intricate three-dimensional representations using specialized software. In this role, individuals will actively engage in the creation of detailed virtual models that simulate real-world objects or environments. Utilizing a variety of techniques and tools within the realm of 3D modeling, these professionals will define shapes, textures, and, when applicable, the dynamic behaviors of digital elements.

We are in search of Product Development and Engineering professionals responsible for guiding the comprehensive process of bringing innovative products to fruition. In this role, individuals will actively contribute to the entire product development lifecycle, collaborating with cross-functional teams to design, prototype, and optimize products for market success.

We are currently seeking CAD Drafting and Documentation professionals who will be responsible for creating precise technical drawings and comprehensive documentation essential for the product development and engineering process. In this role, individuals will utilize Computer-Aided Design (CAD) software to translate conceptual designs into detailed, accurate blueprints and schematics.

We are actively seeking Reverse Engineering specialists who possess the expertise to dissect, analyze, and comprehend the inner workings of existing products, systems, or software. In this role, individuals will employ reverse engineering techniques to unravel the design, functionality, and components of complex structures when original documentation is unavailable or incomplete.

We are currently seeking professionals with expertise in Prototyping and Rapid Manufacturing, integral components of the product development process. In this role, individuals will play a key role in transforming concepts into tangible prototypes and facilitating accelerated manufacturing processes.
