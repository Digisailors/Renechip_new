3D modeling and design

3D modeling and design refer to the process of creating three-dimensional representations of objects or scenes using computer software. This technique is widely used in various industries, including animation, video games, virtual reality, architecture, product design, and more.

Product development and engineering

Product development and engineering refer to the processes involved in creating new products or improving existing ones. These processes typically encompass a series of stages, from conceptualization and design to manufacturing and market launch.

CAD drafting and documentation

CAD drafting and documentation refer to the process of creating detailed technical drawings and related documentation using computer-aided design (CAD) software.They are crucial in various industries, including architecture, engineering, manufacturing, and construction.

Reverse engineering

Reverse engineering is the process of deconstructing or analyzing a product, system, or software to understand its design, functionality, and inner workings. This is typically done to gain insights into how a particular technology or product functions, without necessarily having access to its original design or source code.

Prototyping and rapid manufacturing

Prototyping is the process of creating a preliminary or working model of a product to test and validate design concepts, functionalities, and features before full-scale production.Rapid manufacturing refers to the quick and efficient production of physical parts or products using advanced manufacturing technologies and processes.
